About the Challenge

Learn more about the mission, goals, and opportunities offered by the Change the World University Challenge

Our Mission

The Change the World University Challenge is more than a competition—it’s a movement. Our mission is to empower university students worldwide to collaborate on bold, innovative solutions that address pressing global challenges. By fostering creativity, leveraging advanced technologies, and enabling Generative Collective Intelligence (GCI), we aim to catalyze systemic change that transforms lives and communities.

Our Goals

  1. Unleash Student Potential
    • Harness the creativity, energy, and passion of university students to solve real-world problems.
    • Provide tools, resources, and platforms that enable impactful, scalable solutions.
  2. Promote Collaboration and Innovation
    • Shift the paradigm from isolated efforts to networked cooperation that amplifies collective impact.
    • Introduce students to cutting-edge concepts like the Technology Gravity Well, Collective Social Brain, and Strong Collective Intelligence to frame their problem-solving approaches.
  3. Multiply Impact with Generative Collective Intelligence
    • Equip participants with the GCI platform to discover networks of interventions that collaborate to achieve exponential impact, rather than focusing on isolated, competitive solutions.
  4. Create Lasting Change
    • Support sustainable development and inclusive growth by addressing systemic challenges such as poverty, inequality, climate change, and more.

Why This Challenge is Different

Traditional competitions focus on single solutions competing for limited impact. The Change the World University Challenge is different.

  • Networked Cooperation: We encourage participants to explore how multiple interventions can cooperate to multiply impact.
  • Generative Collective Intelligence App: A game-changing platform helps participants form teams, identify shared goals, and discover scalable networks of cooperation.
  • Real-World Application: Projects are designed to solve practical challenges with measurable outcomes, empowering participants to create lasting change.

Opportunities for Participants

  1. Phase 0: Share Your Ideas
    • Start your journey by exploring groundbreaking concepts like the Collective Social Brain, Technology Gravity Well, and Strong Collective Intelligence.
    • Submit short essays or multimedia content to generate meaningful engagement and win prizes.
  2. Phases 1-10: Build and Scale Solutions
    • Use the Generative Collective Intelligence App to form or join teams, discover collaborators, and design networks of interventions that multiply impact.
    • Tackle targeted challenges in areas like sustainable development, education, and healthcare.
  3. Skill Development and Learning
    • Gain hands-on experience in collaborative innovation, systems thinking, and cutting-edge technologies.
    • Access courses, mentorships, and tools to enhance your skills and amplify your impact.
  4. Recognition and Impact
    • Win prizes, gain global visibility, and leave a lasting legacy by contributing to transformative change.
    • Be featured as a success story, inspiring others to follow your lead.

Who Can Participate?

The challenge is open to all university students—anyone with a bold idea, a collaborative mindset, and a passion for making a difference. Whether you’re an individual looking for a team or part of a group ready to dive in, there’s a place for you in the Change the World University Challenge.

Join the Movement

Be part of something extraordinary. The Change the World University Challenge is your opportunity to combine your creativity, skills, and vision with the power of collective intelligence to solve the world’s most pressing challenges.
